Q: Who is Metro Retro Furniture

A: Metro Retro started in 2000 as an online store providing designer furniture and accessories for thousands of customers through eBay. Demand from our customers led us to acquire a 30,000 sq. foot warehouse in Pasadena, Texas, and more recently a 5000 sq. foot showroom in the Montrose area of Houston.

We feature vintage items from Herman Miller, Jens Risom, Knoll, and Dunbar. We are the Houston representative for Modernica, Gordon International, Zographos and select Italian manufacturers of classic furniture. Metro Retro ships from Texas to locations throughout the United States, Asia, Great Britain & Europe, and the Americas. Metro Retro also offers artwork from corporate collections.

Q: What kind of stuff do you sell?

A: We specialize in vintage Mid Century Modern designer pieces typically designed and manufactured between 1950-1975. We also offer new reproduction pieces of classic mid century designs. All of our vintage pieces are in good used condition. Brand new items are indicated in the item description. We also have a full service in-house wood refinishing shop and we can make most used pieces look as good as new.

Q: How does Metro Retro rate the condition of furniture?

A: Most of our pieces are vintage (30+ year old) furniture. Condition will vary. A little bruising on chair and table legs is normal wear for furniture of that age. We will point out more-than-normal bruising on a piece, however.

Metro Retro uses the following condition ratings:

Mint The piece looks like it came off a showroom floor. It has all of its original finish, with original luster and no wear or flaking. There are no nicks, gouges, or scratches. Any discoloration is from the manufacturer and not a later stain.
Excellent Nearly showroom perfect with one or two superficial blemishes. Overall appearance is not affected by blemishes. Piece may be refinished. We will note in the description if it has been refinished or reupholstered.
Very Good Showing some signs of age. Superficial surface scratches and blemishes are more visible than Excellent. Finish may have minor flaking but the piece is otherwise presentable. A good cleaning or polishing will improve appearance.
Good Definite signs of age and use. May have visible scratches and stains but piece will be useable without refinishing. May have repairs.
Poor Major signs of age and wear. Will probably need to be refinished and/or repaired. A good candidate for a restoration project.
